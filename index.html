HOW TO PLAY
🖱 PLACE TOWERS — Select a tower from the panel, then click on green grid cells to place it.
⚡ EARN GOLD — Defeat enemies to earn gold. Use gold to buy & upgrade towers.
🌊 WAVES — Click "SEND WAVE" to launch the next alien invasion.
❤️ LIVES — Enemies reaching your base cost lives. Lose all 20 = game over.
🔧 UPGRADES — Click a placed tower to upgrade or sell it.
💥 POWER-UPS — Use emergency abilities to turn the tide of battle.

KEYBOARD SHORTCUTS:
[1-5] Select tower · [Space] Send wave · [Esc] Deselect
